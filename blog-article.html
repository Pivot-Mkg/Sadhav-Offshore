Case Study

Revolutionizing Deepwater Platform Maintenance in the Arabian Sea

Published on August 15, 2024 8 min read
Our team conducting maintenance on the deepwater platform in the Arabian Sea

Key Takeaways

  • 40% reduction in maintenance downtime
  • 30% cost savings compared to traditional methods
  • Zero safety incidents during 120-day operation
  • Implementation of innovative ROV technology

Project Overview

In early 2024, Sadhav Offshore was contracted to perform critical maintenance on a deepwater production platform in the Arabian Sea. The aging infrastructure required extensive work to extend its operational life while minimizing production downtime.

Challenges Faced

  • Harsh weather conditions with waves up to 4 meters
  • Limited operational windows due to environmental restrictions
  • Complex underwater structural repairs at 120m depth
  • Strict environmental protection requirements

Our Innovative Approach

Our team implemented a three-phase strategy that combined cutting-edge technology with our deep industry expertise:

1. Advanced Inspection

Utilized autonomous underwater vehicles (AUVs) equipped with high-definition cameras and sensors to conduct a comprehensive structural assessment without human divers.

2. Robotic Repairs

Deployed remotely operated vehicles (ROVs) with specialized tooling to perform precise repairs on underwater structures, significantly reducing diver risk and downtime.

3. Predictive Maintenance

Installed IoT sensors throughout the platform to enable real-time monitoring and predictive maintenance, preventing future unplanned shutdowns.

Results Achieved

30%
Cost savings vs traditional methods
0
Safety incidents in 120 days

"Sadhav Offshore's innovative approach and technical expertise allowed us to complete critical maintenance with minimal disruption to our operations. Their commitment to safety and efficiency is unmatched in the industry."

Looking Forward

This project has set a new standard for offshore maintenance operations. The successful implementation of advanced technologies and methodologies has not only benefited this specific platform but has also provided valuable insights that we're now applying to similar projects across the region.
