Revolutionizing Deepwater Platform Maintenance in the Arabian Sea

August 15, 2024 8 min read

In early 2024, Sadhav Offshore was contracted to perform critical maintenance on a deepwater production platform in the Arabian Sea. The aging infrastructure required extensive work to extend its operational life while minimizing production downtime.

Our team conducting maintenance on the deepwater platform in the Arabian Sea

Key Takeaways

  • 40% reduction in maintenance downtime
  • 30% cost savings compared to traditional methods
  • Zero safety incidents during 120-day operation
  • Implementation of innovative ROV technology

Challenges Faced

Harsh Weather Conditions

Operating in waves up to 4 meters required specialized equipment and careful planning to ensure crew safety and operational continuity.

Complex Underwater Repairs

Structural repairs at 120m depth demanded advanced ROV technology and highly skilled operators to ensure precision work in challenging conditions.

Our Innovative Approach

1

Advanced Inspection

Utilized autonomous underwater vehicles (AUVs) equipped with high-definition cameras and sensors to conduct comprehensive structural assessments without human divers, significantly reducing risk and downtime.
