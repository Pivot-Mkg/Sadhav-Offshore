Service Partner to Kongsberg Maritime

Sadhav is proud to be associated with Kongsberg Maritime for the services on its Kamewa waterjets installed on Indian Coastguard Ships. Kongsberg Maritime is a world leader in Maritime technology having an extensive portfolio of products.

Sadhav has signed the Service Partner agreement with Rolls Royce Marine AS (Now Kongsberg Maritime) in 2018. Subsequently, Sadhav's service teams have undergone extensive training, both at Factory in Sweden and On-the-Job training to undertake the Repairs & Maintenance,Routines of the Kamewa Waterjet propulsion systems.

Now appointed by Indian Coastguard as PAC Representative of Kongsberg Maritime, Sadhav service teams are undertaking Defect rectification and Routine Maintenance on the Kongsberg Kamewa S2, S3 Waterjets fitted on ICG Ships operating on the Eastern Seaboard and Andaman & Nicobar region.
