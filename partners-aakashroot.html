CBAF - Copper-Based Antifouling (ICAF/MGPS)

CBAF protects sea chests, strainers, and box coolers by releasing controlled copper ions that inhibit early-stage marine growth. By disrupting nutrient uptake at the cellular level, the system effectively reduces fouling and preserves heat-exchange performance.

UAF - Ultrasonic Antifouling

Ultrasonic Antifouling offers an eco-friendly, low-maintenance alternative to copper systems. Tuned ultrasonic frequencies prevent biofilm formation on wetted surfaces without harming marine life. Developed with marine biologists, the method reduces downtime and operating costs while remaining environmentally considerate.

ICCP - Impressed Current Cathodic Protection

ICCP replaces sacrificial anodes with a controlled, power-assisted system that delivers consistent hull protection. While the initial investment is higher, ICCP provides strong lifetime ROI through reduced drag, fewer anode replacements, and stable corrosion control across operating environments.

Micro ICCP

  • Compact ICCP engineered for smaller vessels at lower lifecycle cost.
  • Downsized anodes and low-amp rectifiers deliver optimal protection with minimal maintenance.
  • Reduces hull drag and supports improved fuel efficiency; environmentally considerate.
  • Rapidly becoming the preferred choice for small craft across applications.

SA - Sacrificial (Galvanic) Anodes

We supply and service sacrificial anodes for vessels, ports, and offshore assets. By attaching a more reactive metal to the structure, galvanic anodes corrode preferentially, shielding the hull and systems. Proper specification and placement ensure continuous protection throughout service intervals.
