Our Story

Founded in 1992, Sadhav Offshore has grown from a local service provider to a respected international player in the maritime industry.

Our journey is one of relentless innovation, strategic growth, and an unwavering focus on client satisfaction. We pride ourselves on adapting to the ever-changing demands of the offshore world while staying true to our core principles of safety, quality, and integrity.

Group Companies

Through our expanding team of skilled professionals, modern assets, and trusted associates, we continue to build a strong network of satisfied clients across diverse sectors of the maritime industry.

Sadhav Shipping Ltd.

Sadhav Shipping, founded in 1996, owns and operates more than 25 marine vessels across multiple sectors, providing reliable solutions and quality service in the Indian maritime trade.

Read More

Sadhav Offshore Engineering

Established in 1992, Sadhav Offshore delivers core engineering services to upstream oil and gas, shipbuilding, dry docking, ship repair, and OEM representation, ensuring efficiency and reliability.

Read More

Sadhav Drydocks Pvt. Ltd.

Sadhav Drydocks serves as the group’s investment arm, leading the development of an integrated dry dock and ship repair complex in Paradip, Odisha, to strengthen India’s maritime infrastructure.
